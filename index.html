what we do

We provide Paediatric Occupational Therapy for children aged 0-12 years. Our sessions are delivered weekly, face-to-face, at our new clinic located in Emu Heights, NSW. We focus on play-based activities aimed at enhancing and developing skills in the following areas:

  • Sensory processing
  • Emotional regulation
  • Play, social, and problem-solving skills
  • Behaviour management
  • Fine motor skills
  • Gross motor skills
  • Attention and concentration
  • Independent self-care
  • School readiness
